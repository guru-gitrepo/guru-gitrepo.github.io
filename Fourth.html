Higher Education - A complete redundant ?

Back
One thing has been revealed during covid-19 pandemic is that Higher Education is totally a waste and redundant. During last 2 years, all classes in the colleges were carried out in on-line mode. I know as a teacher, "On-line class" is a big scam, in which neither a teacher really can teach nor a student can learn; It is a scheme where giant companies, which provide internet service and video call platforms can become even more rich. So these 2 years, there has been absolute zero teaching-learning happened. But if you obseve, this has not effected the graduates even in a smallest possible way in getting jobs. In fact, in Engineering colleges almost all got placed with good package during pandemic time which was not happening in pre-Covid era. Also, there has not been any ill effect in the industries where these grduates got jobs. This proves one thing- Except a few kind of jobs like medical or law, None of the white collar jobs need graduates; Only 12th class completed students are sufficient. I storngly suggest government policy makers, instead of spending a huge resource in higher education, it is wise, if that money is put into primary and secondary school education and improve the quality. I insist IT companies to hire 12th passed students, Since there are very young you can train them efficiently and easily. Only, the students who are really knowledge hungry should go to the college. Then only a graduation will have some meaning. If people start working from the age of 18, it improves the GDP of a nation and the professional responsibility will chanel youth energy in good things. Once this happens, No one can use higher education system as a commerical product to make money.
