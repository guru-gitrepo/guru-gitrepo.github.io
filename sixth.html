I have created an excel sheet template for calculating income tax for the financial year 2025-26 under ITR-2. It assumes following -

  1. You are a citizen of India.
  2. Your total incomer under normal slab is less than or equal to 16 lakh.
  3. You have capital gain (LTCG/STCG) from shares and/or mutual funds.
